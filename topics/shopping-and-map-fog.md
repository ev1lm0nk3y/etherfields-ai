# Topic: Shopping Modifiers & Map Grid Fog of War

This topic covers **Shopping card modifications** and rules regarding **locked Map Tiles (Fog of War)** on the Dreamworld Map.

---

## 1. Shopping Node Market Modifiers
By default, when standing on a Shopping node and resolving the Shopping Card (11-41C), each Dreamer draws the top **3 cards** of the **Influence Market Deck** to buy cards with Ether (pg 15).

* **Advanced Upgrades:** Certain acquired Influence cards or campaign events alter this baseline setup step.
* **Usage:** Activating a modifier card that increases your market size (e.g., "draw 5 cards instead of 3") allows you to draw a wider **5-card display** for your Shopping display. This gives the team a greater variety of advanced cards to choose from when spending their Ether.

---

## 2. Dreamworld Map Locked Tiles (Fog of War)
The Dreamworld Map is an evolving bird's eye map that expands as the campaign progresses.

* **Unreachable Map Tiles:** You **cannot** walk through, travel across, or access grid coordinates or tiles that have not been physically placed or unlocked.
* **Dream Gate Fog of War:** It is completely normal to receive a **Dream Gate Tile** for a location that is currently hidden under the "fog of war" (i.e., its map tiles are not yet unlocked or placed on the board).
* **Unlocking Nodes:** To reach these distant Dream Gates, you must scout adjacent tiles, perform **Exploration Actions**, or resolve **Secret Scripts** to unlock and connect the missing Map Tiles, physically forming a walkable path to the Gate.
