# Topic: Spirit Tokens & Character Specifics (The Free Spirit)

This topic covers **Spirit tokens** and rules unique to **The Free Spirit** character archetype.

---

## 1. Physical Components
* **Spirit Tokens:** There are exactly **5 Spirit tokens** in the core box (Rulebook v2.0, pg 3). They feature a mystical smoke/vapor icon and are distinct from standard universal, distress, or personal tokens.
* **Exclusivity:** Spirit tokens are **exclusive** to **The Free Spirit** character archetype. If no one is playing the Free Spirit, these tokens remain in the box.

---

## 2. Gameplay Mechanics & Usage
* **Resource Generation:** The Free Spirit generates Spirit tokens through specific triggers on her cards, often involving **Sealing** her own cards or utilizing her special soul-over-body abilities.
* **Intent Conversion:** These tokens act as a customizable, temporary resource pool. The Free Spirit can cash them in during encounters to generate raw **Intent** ($\Delta$) or bypass typical mechanical limits.
