# Topic: Boss Slumbers (The Butcher/Tormentor) & Passive Slumbers (The Dream Monger)

This topic covers the specialized setup and rules for unique Slumber encounters, specifically **The Butcher** (using the custom **Tormentor** tracker) and **The Dream Monger** (a passive survival/timer Slumber).

---

## 1. Unique Boss Trackers: The Butcher & "Use Tormentor"
Some powerful boss Slumber cards do not use standard numbered Turn decks. Instead, they specify **"Use Tormentor"** in their setup.

* **Turn Replacement:** This completely replaces the standard numbered Turn Deck with a specialized oversized card or boss tracker titled **"Tormentor"** (found in your oversized Entity/Slumber/Turn cards section).
* **Placement:** Place the Tormentor card face-up in the Turn Deck slot of the board.
* **Rules of Time:** The Tormentor track alters how turns and time progress. Follow the printed instructions on the Tormentor card to see what triggers a "tick" or movement on its custom track.

---

## 2. Passive Slumbers: The Dream Monger
The Dream Monger Slumber is a passive survival encounter that functions as a timer-based puzzle.

### Setup & Play:
* **Baseline Tiles:** Setup the standard starting **II-36 A** Slumber Map tiles on B2 and B3.
* **Turn Deck:** Shuffle and place exactly **2 Turn cards** face-up on the Turn deck slot.
* **Passive Entity:** The Entity has no printed actions and does nothing during the Dreamscape phase.
* **Resolution / Escape:** To successfully conclude the Slumber, the Dreamers must either:
  1. Pay for a manual **escape** Map Action printed on the tiles or cards using required Intent.
  2. Simply survive and pass their turns until the 2 Turn cards are completely exhausted (running out of turns triggers the Slumber's "Out of Turns/Skip" victory or penalty passage).
