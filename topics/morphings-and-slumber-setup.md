# Topic: Morphing Tokens & Slumber Map Setup

This topic covers rules for **Morphing tokens** (nightmare modifications) and the baseline setup for **Slumber Map Decks**.

---

## 1. Morphing Tokens (Nightmare Mutations)
Morphings represent random mutations that attach to an Entity, making them progressively stronger.

* **Components:** Morphing tokens are **small, circular cardboard tokens** (Rulebook v2.0, pg 3). They are distinct from the triangular Sign tokens and rectangular State tokens.
* **Acquisition:** When a card or effect dictates "Add 1 Morphing", draw a Morphing token randomly from a cup/bag and place it face-up on the targeted Entity card. These stack and multiply modifiers (e.g., +1 Movement, +1 Attack Range, or forcing players to Seal cards when hit).
* **Fending Off (Universal Basic Action):** Any Dreamer can perform the **Remove Morphing** action (pg 9, 20):
  * **Cost:** Spend **1 Key** OR **1 Shining Gem** ($\approx$).
  * **Condition:** The targeted Entity must be within **Range 1** (adjacent or in the same space).
  * **Result:** Discard one Morphing token of your choice from the Entity back into the global pool.

---

## 2. Slumber Map Deck Setup (Fog of War)
When setting up Slumber encounters, the layout is highly structured.

* **Standard Starting Layout:** Every standard Slumber encounter begins with *only* the **two baseline II-36 A map tiles** placed face-up in spaces **B2 and B3** (pg 14).
* **The Slumber Map Tiles Deck:** All other unlocked Slumber Map tiles form a shuffled, face-down deck kept on the side of the board.
* **Dynamic Expansion:** You do **not** setup the other tiles at the start of a Slumber. They are only drawn and placed to expand the board dynamically when a specific **Fate card** or **Entity effect** explicitly commands it.
* **Awakening Cleanup:** Upon concluding a Dream or during Awakening, all Slumber Map tiles on the board are cleared, and the board resets back to the baseline two II-36 A tiles (pg 14).
