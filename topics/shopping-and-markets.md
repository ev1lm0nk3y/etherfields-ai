# Topic: Shopping & Markets (Rulebook v2.0, pg 15)

This topic covers how the **Shopping** mechanic works when players arrive at a **Shopping Location** on the Dreamworld Map.

---

## 1. Overview & Purpose
Shopping allows players to spend accumulated **Ether** ($\approx$) to upgrade their personal decks or purchase useful equipment for their active inventory:
* **Influence Market:** Upgrades a player's hand/influence deck options.
* **Item Market:** Upgrades a player's active physical tools/items.

---

## 2. Currency
* **Ether ($\approx$ or $\propto$):** The standard currency of the Dreamworld.
* **Sellers Remorse / Pass Reward:** If a Dreamer chooses not to purchase any card or item, they receive **1 Ether** from the pool.

---

## 3. Shopping Options (Resolution Step-by-Step)
When a player lands on a Shopping Location, they must expend the **Shopping Card (11-41C)**. Each Dreamer may then select **one** of the following options:

### Option A: Buy Influence Cards
1. Draw **3 cards** from the **Influence Market Deck**.
2. Pay the individual **Ether cost** in the top right of each card you wish to purchase.
3. Place purchased cards into your personal **discard pile** (they will be shuffled into your deck during future setups).
4. Return any unbought cards to the **bottom of the Market deck**.

### Option B: Buy exactly 1 Item
1. Draw **1 random card** from the **Item Market**.
2. Pay flat **2 Ether**.
3. Place the card into an **Active Item slot** on the board.
   * *Note:* Up to **3 Active Items** can be kept at once. If your slots are full, you must discard either an existing active item or the newly drawn item to your **Storage**.

### Option C: Pass / No Purchase
* If a Dreamer chooses not to buy any cards/items, they **gain 1 Ether**.

---

## 4. Resolution Completion
After all players resolve their Shopping action, return the **Shopping Card (11-41C)** back to the **Secrets** deck/box.
