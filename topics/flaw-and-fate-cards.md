# Topic: Flaw Cards & Fate Cards Rules & Corrections

This topic covers the critical rules regarding **Flaw cards** and **Fate cards**, including major corrections to previous incorrect gameplay explanations.

---

## 1. ⚠️ CRITICAL CORRECTION: Flaw Cards (Decks & Clogging)
* **PREVIOUS INCORRECT EXPLANATION:** You were previously told that Flaw cards do not permanently clog your deck and are discarded directly to the global Flaw Deck Discard Pile.
* **THE CORRECT RULE (Rulebook v2.0, pg 15):** 
  * **Flaw cards ABSOLUTELY clog your personal deck permanently** until you pay a specific removal price to get rid of them.
  * **Acquisition:** When instructed to gain a Flaw card, draw one from the global Flaw Deck and place it **on the top of your personal Discard Pile**.
  * **Clogging & Setup:** During any deckbuilding or setup step, **all** Flaw cards in your possession **must** be added back into your Influence Deck (they do not count toward your standard 20-40 card deck limit).
  * **Resolution:** Whenever you draw a Flaw card, you must resolve its negative effect **immediately**. After resolution, it goes back to your **personal Discard Pile** (or into your Progress Zone as a persistent negative modifier), meaning it will cycle back into your hand repeatedly.
  * **Removal:** To permanently remove a Flaw card from your deck, you must pay its printed removal price (usually **1 Ether** $\approx$, or as specified on the card). Only after paying this cost is the Flaw card returned to the **bottom of the global Flaw Deck**.

---

## 2. ⚠️ CORRECTION: Fate Cards Duration
* **PREVIOUS INCORRECT EXPLANATION:** You were previously told that all drawn Fate cards stay in effect face-up on the table until a specific discard condition is met.
* **THE CORRECT RULE (Rulebook v2.0, pg 15):** 
  * **Most Fate cards are temporary, one-time events.**
  * **Resolution:** When you enter a Fate location, draw a Fate card, resolve its text immediately, and then **discard it to the bottom of the Fate deck**.
  * **Exceptions (Quirks):** A Fate card only stays in play if it has a **Quirk** on it, and there is an empty **Quirk slot** on one of the active Slumber Map tiles on the board. In this case, place the Fate card into that slot where it will apply a persistent positive or negative rule to the Dreamscape. It remains there until a rule explicitly removes it or the Slumber ends.
