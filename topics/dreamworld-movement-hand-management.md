# Topic: Traveling, Hand Management, and End-of-Dream Reset & Shortcuts

This topic covers Traveling on the **Dreamworld Map**, deck reset conditions, and how to resolve **Shortcuts & Map Actions**, including critical corrections to previous gameplay explanations.

---

## 1. ⚠️ CRITICAL CORRECTION: End-of-Dream Reset & Hand Management
* **PREVIOUS INCORRECT EXPLANATION:** You were previously told that after successfully completing a Dream, your deck resets, you discard your hand to zero, and you do not hold physical cards while traveling on the Dreamworld Map.
* **THE CORRECT RULE (Rulebook v2.0, pg 13, 15):**
  * **No Automatic Resets:** Successfully completing a Slumber or Dream **DOES NOT** trigger a deck shuffle or reset. 
  * **Keep Your Hand:** You **do not discard your hand**, active Progress cards, or Shining Gems when concluding a Dream. You keep them exactly as they are on the table and in your hand.
  * **No Automatic Shuffling:** You do **not** shuffle your personal discard pile back into your deck. 
  * **Holding Cards on the Map:** You **do hold and play physical cards** while traveling on the Dreamworld Map. In fact, ending Slumbers/Dreams with a strong hand is a key strategy, as certain Influence and Item cards can be played directly on the Dreamworld Map to aid in movement or map resolution (pg 15).
  * **When Do Decks Reset?** A complete deck reset and reshuffle (returning all hand/discard cards and resolving Distress/Sealed recovery) **only** occurs during **Awakening** (pg 13) or when resolving the **Delta Phase** (pg 15).

---

## 2. ⚠️ CRITICAL CORRECTION: Map Shortcuts & Sealing
* **PREVIOUS INCORRECT EXPLANATION:** You were previously told that taking a shortcut on the map costs "2 cards from hand/deck" and that you pay by flipping the top 2 cards of your deck.
* **THE CORRECT RULE (Rulebook v2.0, pg 14):**
  * **Shortcut Cost:** Moving along a red-bordered Shortcut arrow on the Dreamworld Map has a flat cost: **Each Dreamer must Seal 1 card**.
  * **How to Seal (pg 8):** Sealing is **never** done from hand. To Seal 1 card, you reveal the **top card of your face-down Influence Deck** and place it into your personal Sealed pile (under your Dreamer board). You do not discard cards or flip cards to your discard pile for shortcuts.
  * **Flaw Exemption:** If the revealed card is a Flaw card, it is bypassed (not sealed), and you draw the next non-Flaw card to Seal instead. Put the Flaw card back on top of your deck.
  * **Deck Exhaustion:** If your deck is empty when you are forced to Seal, you do **not** suffer any penalty and you do **not** reshuffle (pg 8).

---

## 3. Paying for Standard Map Actions
* Because you **do keep your hand** on the Dreamworld Map, any other standard Map Actions or Fate events that require discarding or spending Intent are paid normally from your active hand or active Item cards.
* If your Influence Deck runs out while drawing cards (due to a rare map drawing effect), you must reshuffle your Discard Pile to form a new deck and suffer the standard **Reshuffle Penalty** (suffer 1 Distress OR Seal 3 cards from your deck).
